About this property

Perched above the winding lanes of El Born, The Harbour View Suite is a 19th-century merchant's townhouse restored with obsessive care. Original wrought-iron balconies frame a panorama that stretches from the Barceloneta beach to the glittering Mediterranean — the kind of view that makes you push breakfast back an hour.

Each of the four SnapRooms has been individually designed by Barcelona-based studio Atelier Nova. Think hand-plastered walls in Saharan white, salvaged timber beams, and bespoke furniture made by local artisans. There's nothing hotel-generic here — every corner has been considered. The rooftop terrace is shared between guests and comes with a plunge pool, a fire pit, and 360-degree views of the Gothic Quarter skyline.
