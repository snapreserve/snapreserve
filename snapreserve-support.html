🏨 For Guests

Everything you need to know before, during, and after your stay.

How do I cancel my booking? ▼
Go to your Trips page, select the booking you want to cancel, and click "Cancel booking." If you cancel more than 48 hours before check-in, you'll receive a full refund. If you cancel within 48 hours, you'll receive a 50% refund. Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days depending on your bank.
⏰ Free cancellation deadline: 48 hours before your check-in time
Can I change my check-in or checkout dates? ▼
Yes — go to your Trips page, select the booking, and click "Request date change." Your host will need to approve the new dates. If the new dates are cheaper, you'll be refunded the difference. If they're more expensive, you'll be charged the extra amount. Date changes are subject to availability.
I can't check in — what do I do? ▼
First, message your host directly through the app — they usually respond within minutes. If you can't reach your host within 30 minutes of your expected check-in time, contact us at support@snapreserve.co or use the urgent help button. We'll step in immediately and either resolve the issue or arrange alternative accommodation at no extra cost.
How long does a refund take? ▼
Once a refund is approved, it typically takes 5–10 business days to appear on your original payment method. Credit cards usually process faster (3–5 days). Debit cards can take up to 10 days. You'll receive an email confirmation as soon as the refund is initiated.
The property wasn't as described — what can I do? ▼
If your property significantly differs from the listing — missing amenities, incorrect room type, safety issues, or cleanliness problems — contact us within 24 hours of check-in. Include photos where possible. We take these seriously and will either resolve the issue with the host or arrange a full refund and alternative accommodation.
Can I leave a review after my stay? ▼
Yes — you'll receive an email after checkout with a link to leave a review. You can also go to your Trips page and click "Leave a review" on any past booking. Reviews can be left up to 14 days after checkout. All reviews are verified — only guests who actually stayed can leave them.
What is the SnapReserve service fee? ▼
SnapReserve charges a 3.2% service fee on every booking — one of the lowest in the industry. This fee covers payment processing, 24/7 customer support, fraud protection, and platform maintenance. The fee is shown clearly in the price breakdown before you confirm any booking. There are no hidden charges.
🏠 For Hosts

Everything you need to manage your listing and get paid reliably.

When do I get paid? ▼
Payouts are released 24 hours after your guest checks in. This gives us time to confirm there are no issues. Payments are transferred directly to your bank account via Stripe. Standard bank transfers take 2–5 business days. You can track all your payouts in your Host Dashboard.
✓ No payout fees — you keep 100% of your listing price minus the 3.2% platform fee
How do I edit or pause my listing? ▼
Log into your Host Dashboard, go to "My Listings," and click the listing you want to edit. You can update photos, description, pricing, availability, and house rules at any time. To temporarily pause bookings, toggle "Pause listing" — your listing will be hidden from search results until you reactivate it.
A guest cancelled — what happens to my payout? ▼
It depends on when they cancelled. If the guest cancelled more than 48 hours before check-in, you won't receive a payout for that booking. If they cancelled within 48 hours of check-in, you receive 50% of the booking value. If they were a no-show without cancelling, you receive the full payout.
How do I handle a difficult guest? ▼
First try to resolve issues directly through the messaging system. If you're unable to resolve things, contact us at support@snapreserve.co. In cases of property damage, you can raise a damage claim within 72 hours of checkout through your dashboard. We mediate all disputes fairly between hosts and guests.
Can I block certain dates? ▼
Yes — in your Host Dashboard, go to your listing's availability calendar. Click any date or drag across a range to block it. Blocked dates won't appear as available to guests. You can also set minimum and maximum stay lengths, and apply different rates for weekends or peak seasons.
What are the host requirements? ▼
To list on SnapReserve you need: a verified ID, a valid bank account for payouts, accurate photos of your property, and a response rate above 70% within 24 hours. Properties must comply with local short-term rental laws. We verify all listings before they go live — this usually takes 1–2 business days.
💳 Payments & Refunds

How money moves on SnapReserve — transparent, simple, fair.

🔒
Secure payments
All payments are processed by Stripe — the same payment infrastructure used by Amazon, Airbnb, and Shopify. Your card details are never stored on our servers.
💸
Refund timeline
Refunds are initiated immediately upon approval. Allow 5–10 business days for your bank to process it. You'll get an email confirmation as soon as we send it.
📊
Service fee breakdown
3.2% platform fee, shown before you pay. Covers payment processing, support, fraud protection. No booking fees, no hidden charges.
Lowest fee in the industry
🏦
Host payouts
Released 24h after guest check-in. Direct bank transfer via Stripe. 2–5 business days to arrive. No payout fees — you receive the full booking amount minus the 3.2% platform fee.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Payments are processed in USD. Currency conversion is handled automatically for international cards by your card issuer.
Why was my payment declined? ▼
Common reasons include: insufficient funds, card issuer blocking the transaction (call your bank to authorise), incorrect card details, or an expired card. Try a different card or contact your bank. If the issue persists, email us at support@snapreserve.co.
Is my payment information safe? ▼
Yes. SnapReserve never stores your card details. All payment data is handled directly by Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 certified — the highest level of payment security certification. All transactions are encrypted using TLS 1.3.

What counts as exceptional circumstances? ▼
Exceptional circumstances include: serious illness or injury requiring hospitalisation, death of a close family member, natural disasters or severe weather making travel impossible, government travel bans, and major transport disruptions beyond your control. Documentation will be required. Contact us as soon as possible at support@snapreserve.co.
👤 Account & Profile

Managing your SnapReserve account, identity, and preferences.

How do I verify my identity? ▼
Go to your profile settings and click "Verify ID." You'll be asked to upload a government-issued photo ID (passport or driver's licence) and a selfie. Verification is usually completed within 2 hours. Verified accounts get a blue checkmark and are prioritised in search results.
How do I delete my account? ▼
Go to Profile → Settings → Account → "Delete account." Please note: you cannot delete your account if you have active bookings or pending payouts. All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days in accordance with our Privacy Policy. If you're a host, your listing will be removed immediately.
I forgot my password — how do I reset it? ▼
On the login page, click "Forgot password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. If you don't see it, check your spam folder. The reset link expires after 1 hour. If you continue to have issues, contact support@snapreserve.co.
How do I update my email address? ▼
Go to Profile → Settings → Personal info → "Change email." You'll need to verify both your old and new email addresses. This is a security measure to prevent unauthorised changes. The update takes effect immediately after both addresses are confirmed.
