Section 02 — FAQ
FAQ

Got a
question?

Everything you need to know about booking, hosting, payments, and cancellations. Can't find your answer?

💬 Chat with us →
Are there any hidden fees on SnapReserve?
+
Never. The price you see on the search card is the price you pay — taxes included. We built SnapReserve specifically because we were tired of seeing fees appear at the last step of checkout. Every listing on our platform displays the total nightly price upfront, with a full breakdown available before you confirm.
How is the host trust rating calculated?
+
Our trust rating is a score out of 100, calculated from 6 dimensions: Cleanliness, Accuracy, Communication, Check-in, Location, and Value. It's recalculated after every completed stay using only reviews from verified guests who actually checked in.
Is my payment secure?
+
Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe, the world's most trusted payment infrastructure — used by Amazon, Shopify, and millions of businesses in the US. Your card details are never stored on our servers. Payments are held securely and only released to the host 24 hours after your check-in, giving you time to flag any issues.
When do I get charged and when is my host paid?
+
You're charged at the time of booking. The host receives their payout 24 hours after you successfully check in. This protects you — if the property doesn't match the listing, you can raise a SnapGuarantee claim before the host is paid.
What is the cancellation policy?
+
Each listing has its own cancellation policy set by the host — Flexible, Moderate, or Strict. You'll see it clearly displayed before you confirm. Flexible means full refund up to 24 hours before check-in. Moderate is 5 days. Strict is non-refundable unless the listing is materially inaccurate — in which case the SnapGuarantee applies.
What is the SnapGuarantee?
+
The SnapGuarantee protects every guest on our platform. If a property is significantly different from its listing — wrong location, missing amenities, safety issues — we'll either find you equivalent accommodation at our expense or give you a full refund. Claims must be raised within 24 hours of check-in.
How do I list my property on SnapReserve?
+
Sign up as a host, complete ID verification, connect your bank account via Stripe, and create your listing. It takes around 30 minutes to go live. Our host dashboard includes pricing tools, calendar management, earnings analytics, and a messaging inbox — everything you need to run your property professionally.
What does SnapReserve charge hosts?
+
Our standard platform fee is 3.2% of the booking total — one of the lowest in the industry, and we include far more tooling than most platforms. Long-standing hosts with excellent reviews get reduced fees. We believe hosts who deliver exceptional experiences deserve to keep more of what they earn.
